About Me

I am a language teacher and linguist based in Québec, Canada. I teach English and Brazilian Portuguese at Cégep de Lévis. My previous experience includes positions as a Classics Lecturer at McGill University and Bishop’s University, and as a college teacher at Collège Mérici. I hold an M.A. and a B.A. in Linguistics from the Federal University of Minas Gerais (Brazil), and was enrolled as a doctoral student in Linguistics at UQAM. My teaching covers Portuguese for business, Latin and Ancient Greek terminology, and advanced English grammar. I have been involved in tutoring, language integration for immigrant communities, event organization, student mentoring, and academic outreach.
